1. What was the purpose of Mahatma Gandhi's satyagraha movement?
2. How did Mahatma Gandhi believe the battle against oppression could be won?
3. What event prompted Mahatma Gandhi to launch a nationwide satyagraha against the Rowlatt Act?%%%%1. The purpose of Mahatma Gandhi's satyagraha movement was to fight against injustice and oppression through non-violence and appealing to the conscience of the oppressor.
2. Mahatma Gandhi believed that the battle against oppression could be won through non-violent means, by appealing to the truth and the conscience of the oppressor.
3. The event that prompted Mahatma Gandhi to launch a nationwide satyagraha against the Rowlatt Act was the passing of the act, which granted the government extensive powers to repress political activities and detain political prisoners without trial. Gandhi saw this as an unjust law and called for non-violent civil disobedience against it.
